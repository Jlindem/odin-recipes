Schoby Snack Sandwich Recipe

Description

Unleash your inner detective with the Schooby Snackk Sandwich. A playful combination of peanut butter, grape jelly, and bone-shaped cookies sandwiched between slices of bread, this snack is perfect for solving mysteries or just enjoying a fun, tasty treat. It's as delightful and satisfying as catching the bad guy in a classic cartoon caper.

Ingredients

Serves 1

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Grape jelly
  • Schoby snacks (or dog bone-shaped cookies for the non-canine)

Steps

  1. Spread a generous layer of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Add a layer of grape jelly on another slice.
  3. Place dog bone-shaped cookies in a pattern on the peanut butter.
  4. Combine the two slices, cut diagonally, and solve mysteries.
