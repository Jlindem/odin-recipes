The Hobbit's Second Breakfast Sandwhich Recipe

Ingredients

Serves four

  • 1 largue baguette
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 small wheel of cheese (Brie or Cheddar)
  • Mustard (for that Shire Sting)

Steps

  1. Fry bacon to crispy perfection, set aside on some paper towels to drain.
  2. Scramble eggs with a dash of courage.
  3. Slice baguette lengthwise, layer with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  4. Add bacon and scrambled eggs.
  5. Generously apply mustard, close the sandwich, and cut into hobbit-sized portions.
