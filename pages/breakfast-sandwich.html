The Hobbit's Second Breakfast Sandwhich Recipe

Description

Step into the Shire with this whimsical and hearty Second Breakfast Sandwich. Overflowing with crispy bacon, fluffy scrambled eggs, fresh tomato slices, and rich cheese, all nestled within a fresh baguette, this sandwich is a true Hobbit's delight. It's perfect for fueling up for a day of adventure or enjoying a peaceful moment in your own little corner of Middle-earth.

Ingredients

Serves four

  • 1 largue baguette
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 small wheel of cheese (Brie or Cheddar)
  • Mustard (for that Shire Sting)

Steps

  1. Fry bacon to crispy perfection, set aside on some paper towels to drain.
  2. Scramble eggs with a dash of courage.
  3. Slice baguette lengthwise, layer with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  4. Add bacon and scrambled eggs.
  5. Generously apply mustard, close the sandwich, and cut into hobbit-sized portions.
