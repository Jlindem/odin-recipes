Snowpiercer Protein Sludge Bars Recipe

Description

Experience the gritty survival of the Snowpiercer with these dense, chocolatey Protein Sludge Bars. Made with a mix of protein powder, peanut butter, and a hint of honey, they're the perfect snack for any dystopian adventurer. Their rough texture and dark appearance capture the essence of life on the edge, with each bite offering a taste of resilience and determination.

Ingredients

Serves 200

  • 2 cups protein powder (chocolate for added drama)
  • 1 cup peanut butter (or insect paste for authenticity)
  • 3 tbsp honey (or motor oil for the adventurous)
  • 1/2 cup oats (optional, for texture)
  • A pinch of dystopian despair

Steps

  1. Mix protein powder and oats in a large bowl.
  2. Melt peanut butter and honey together, stir in existential dread.
  3. Combine all ingredients until you achieve a sludgy consistency.
  4. Press into a greased pan and chill until firm. Cut into bars and serve with a side of rebellion.
